Blog One

Sunday, August 21st, 2022 finishing up college makes me feel pretty good about myself. I'm super excited that I have come this far and how well I'm doing in each one of my classes. It's very funny. Never thought I’d end up going. Growing up everyone pushed me to go. Teachers, students, and grandparents, but I was always interested in going, but not knowing for what. The problem with high school is everyone pushes you to go and take something for college. What people don’t sometimes understand is why would I want to go to something I do not desire. why would I go just to be in debt with a degree that is either useless or not enjoyable to me? I'm one of the lucky ones. For years even in high school, I have no idea what my future was capable of. Six almost seven years and here I am. Someone who did the bare minimum. Someone who didn’t care. Someone that is now on the honours list and working very hard to support himself and afford a small apartment on his own with a full-time job and being able to own his own car and drive to where he needs. To be honest I kind of find it not that hard. I didn’t realize you just have to try, and things will work out the way they're supposed to.

Blog Two

Wednesday, August 10th, 2022, staying true to yourself is important. This night could have changed it all for me but recently I came to the realization that I haven’t been. Going out this night was a good start to the night being out with friends, the usual. I was in a very good mood back in my hometown seeing old faces and catching up. I don’t ever enjoy going downtown because I've seen some unwelcoming stuff throughout the years of working at a nightclub and to be honest, I'm not a fan of it, I just attend so I can see people and potentially meet a cool girl. For the first time in a long time, I felt wanted I felt needed but when I decided to expand my night it turned out not to go as I wished it went. Let's just say I made a bad decision that could have changed my life and I'm glad that didn’t happen. But for once when I felt like I was being true to myself, it backfired. I'm grateful for being in the position I am in life but sometimes the grass isn’t always greener on the other side of the fence. You never know what’s going on behind closed doors with anyone no matter who they are, what they do, or how much money they make. It's important to stay true to yourself but always remember it can get the best of you.
